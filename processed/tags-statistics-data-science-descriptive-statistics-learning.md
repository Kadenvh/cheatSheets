Concept: Standard Deviation
Detail: Measure of spread returning units to original scale via square root of variance. Calculate: `np.std(data)`. Manual: `variance ** 0.5`. Z-Score formula: `(x - μ) / σ` gives number of standard deviations from mean. Symbol: σ. Requires variance calculation as prerequisite. Related: Z-Score, outlier detection, normal distribution.
